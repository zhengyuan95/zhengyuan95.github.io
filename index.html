About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, working with Prof. Yuanjin Zheng. I received my Ph.D. in Computational Imaging from NTU in 2024. My research focuses on photoacoustic imaging enhancement, combining model-based optimization with deep learning — including CNN denoiser priors, generative models, and diffusion-based approaches — as well as hardware acceleration for real-time imaging systems. According to Google Scholar, my work has been cited 248 times (h-index 9, i10-index 9).
